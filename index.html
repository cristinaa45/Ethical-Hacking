Ethical Hacking

What is Ethical Hacking?

Ethical hacking is when someone uses hacking techniques to test systems for security problems, but with permission. The goal is to find weaknesses before real attackers do.

Ethical vs Illegal Hacking

The most important difference is authorization. Ethical hackers are hired or given permission to test systems.

Illegal hackers break into systems without permission, which is a crime and can lead to serious consequences.

Why Companies Hire Ethical Hackers

Organizations hire ethical hackers to:

  • Find vulnerabilities before attackers do
  • Test security defenses
  • Improve system protection

This helps prevent data breaches and keeps systems secure.
